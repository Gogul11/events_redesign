Reverse coding

Isn’t it boring writing answers to questions? So why not try something new, how about doing the opposite? Here is a chance to reverse your thinking process. This event requires you to devise a logic for the given set of inputs and outputs. Participate as a team of two or as a lone wolf to excite your brain cells and also win exciting prizes!


Ideathon

To all the young minds buzzing with new and inspiring ideas, Prodigy brings you Ideathon; an event curated for you to present and promote your ideas. Grab this opportunity to showcase your unique project ideas (teams can consist of 1-3 students) to an audience and stand a chance to win exciting prizes.


Decode with Math

Are you a puzzle lover? Do you often find yourself searching for a challenging puzzle to solve? If your answer to the above questions were yes and yes, then we have the perfect event for you! “Decode With Math” is a fun captivating event where you showcase your mathematical and logical skills to solve the given puzzles. Participate individually or as a team of two to SOLVE your way to win exciting prizes!


AMA

Thinking about a career in computer science? Do programming skills play a role in getting into CS? Not sure about what to pursue next? These doubts lead to confusion, don't they? You're in luck because the Ask Me Anything (AMA) session is here for you! Come and interact with our CEG students to get all your doubts clarified!


Connexions

Do you like finding the clues hidden behind images? Do you try to find ‘connexions’ between the most random things that you come across? Here is an opportunity for you to spend an afternoon with us (either alone or as a team of 2) doing the same thing, but with the added incentive of competing with other teams and winning exciting prizes.


General Quiz

Do you consider yourself a ‘wisenheimer’? Do you want to put your intellect to the test in front of your peers? ACM-CEG and Quizzers Anonymous collaborate to bring you 'General Quiz' as a part of Prodigy 2022. Participate as a lone wolf or a team of two and get ready to summon your grey matter.


Shipwreck

As the ship you cruised on slowly descends into the abyss, the Captain of the ship has just ONE life ring for all the people aboard. Do you have what it takes to talk your way out and swim to the shore safely? This is an individual event where contestants will be assigned roles and must persuade the judge why they should be saved from a sinking ship at the expense of the other. Participate in ‘Shipwreck’, where ACM-CEG collaborates with The Literary Club of Anna University to give you an opportunity to convince the judge or perish in the sinking ship. The choice is yours.


Web Development Workshop

Have you found yourself wondering what goes into making a great website? How famous websites like Netflix, Spotify, Google, etc. are brought to life? We all know that “imitation is the sincerest form of flattery”. Hence we present to you the Web Development Workshop, where we will clone the home page of a famous website, thus learning about the development process on the go
