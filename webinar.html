Webinar on Generation Google Scholarship


Applications for Generation Google Scholarship (APAC) for Women in computer science are open! All female students in their second year of study are encouraged to apply. Selected students will receive $1,000 USD for the 2022-2023 school year. Haven’t applied yet? Confused about where to start? Worry not! OffCamp by ACM-CEG is here to help you with the process! OffCamp by ACM-CEG is thrilled to present a webinar on Generation Google Scholarship. Our speaker, Ria Monga, Generation Google Scholar’21, will share her preparation journey with us which will provide insights into the application process,coding tests and interviews. She will also share tips for writing essays and will answer any other questions that you might have about the scholarship!


Thanks for your interest in this webinar by 'OffCamp by ACM-CEG'. The webinar is scheduled to happen on Sunday, 21st November 2021, 3pm IST.
