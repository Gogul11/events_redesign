Meet Our Founder

Dr.Ranjani Parthasarathi, Retired Professor

Dr. Ranjani Parthsarathy is a highly accomplished professional with a diverse educational background, holding a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Madras, an MS in Electrical & Computer Engineering from Illinois Institute of Technology, and a BE (Hons.) in Electronics and Communication Engineering from College of Engineering, Guindy.
With over 27 years at Anna University Chennai and CEG, she excels as a Professor in Computer Science Information Systems. Her expertise includes Computer Architecture, Multicore Computing, Reconfigurable Computing, Networks, and Indian Knowledge Systems and Language Technology. She played a key role in initiating the ACM-CEG student chapter at CEG, guiding its success over nearly two decades. Her instrumental contribution has significantly contributed to the sustained success and growth of this student chapter, fostering a vibrant community of aspiring computer science professionals.
